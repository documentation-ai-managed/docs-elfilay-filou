---
title: Importing Data into grd
description: Step-by-step to import data into Elfilay Filou grd.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Prepare your CSV file">
    Ensure your data is saved as `data.csv`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use the import command">
    ````bash
    grdfilou import --file data.csv
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify import">
    Check the dashboard for your imported records.
  </Step>
</Steps>
