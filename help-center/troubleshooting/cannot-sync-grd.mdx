description: Resolve sync issues in Elfilay Filou grd.
---

## Problem

- `grdfilou sync` fails
- Error: "Unable to reach server"

## Causes

- Network offline
- API token incorrect

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your internet connection">
    ````bash
    ping elfilayfilou.com
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your API token">
    Ensure your token is correct in `~/.grd/config`.
  </Step>
</Steps>
